Paket 6 Hari 5 Malam

RM 2200 (2 orang)

* Hiking Sibayak: RM 100/pax (cash)

Day 1: Pick up Kualanamu Airport → Paten Jaya & Air Bah Manik → Kebun Teh Sidamanik → Simarjarunjung View → Ferry in Samosir → Batu Passa → Check in Hotel Samosir
Day 2: Batu Siallagan → Tomok Palace → Pantai Batu Hoda → Jembatan Tano Ponggol → Holbung Hills → Bukit Sibea-bea → Efrata Waterfall → Stay Hotel Samosir
Day 3: Geosite Sipinsur → Huta Ginjang → Pantai Bul Bul → Khairos Cafe → Bukit Senyum → Pantai Bebas Parapat → Check in Hotel Parapat
Day 4: Sipiso-piso Waterfall → Loken Barn → Paropo – Silalahi → Pasar Buah Berastagi → Gundaling Hill → Check in Hotel Berastagi
Day 5: 04.00 Hiking Sibayak (optional) → Sarune Cafe → Savana Sinabung → Danau Lau Kawar → Sikulikap Waterfall → Sibolang Durian → Check in Hotel Medan
Day 6: Istana Maimoon → Tjong A Fie Mansion → Vihara Medan → Oleh-oleh Medan & Shopping Batik → Drop off Airport → Tour Finish 🙏
